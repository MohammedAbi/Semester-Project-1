Educational Delights for Teachers

As a community-driven museum, we want to collaborate with schools in fostering spaces for learning and exploration. Our dedicated team is prepared to offer your students enriching experiences, including guided tours of the museum, hands-on learning in our laboratory, and engaging video presentations that promise to captivate and inspire.
